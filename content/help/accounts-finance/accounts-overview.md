## Accounts Overview

The accounts domain gives finance staff a centralised view across all billing.

### Receivables
| Page | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| **Timesheets** | Employee timesheets with totals (hours, amounts, on-costs, profit margin) |
| **Invoices** | Contractor invoices |
| **Time Cards** | Coordinator/care partner time cards |
| **Expenses** | Expense claims |

Click the **customer/client name** → receivable detail. Click the **employee/contractor name** → payable detail.

### Payables
Same structure as receivables but from the payment side:
- What we're paying employees (timesheets)
- What we're paying contractors (invoices)
- Care partner/coordinator payments (time cards)
- Travel and expenses
- **Allowances** — all allowances in one table
- **Overtime audit** — flags >38 hours/week or >12 hours/day (SCHADS Award compliance)

### Payments
- **Credit cards** — export file for bank upload
- **NDIS** — export billing file for NDIS + Support at Home funding partners

### Details (Drill-down)
Full line-item detail for timesheets, invoices, and time cards. Export for reconciliation and cross-checking.

### Accounting Dashboard (Xero Sync)
| Section | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| Bills to pay | Pull AP from Xero, backfill line items |
| Invoices | Pull AR from Xero, backfill line items |
| Payroll | Sync employees, expenses, leave, pay runs, pay slips, super |
| Contacts | Bidirectional sync of customers and suppliers |

### FAQ: How do I handle overtime?
The **Overtime Audit** in payables automatically flags shifts exceeding 38 hours/week or 12 hours/day. You can edit rates and add overtime directly on timesheet line items.
