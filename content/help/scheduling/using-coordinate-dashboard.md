## Using the Coordinate Dashboard

The **Coordinate Dashboard** is your operational overview for billing coordination and operations management. Open it by clicking the coordinate icon in the left sidebar, or navigate directly to `/coordinate`.

### Dashboard Sections

The dashboard is divided into two main areas:

### Priority Alerts

These panels highlight items that need immediate attention:

| Panel | What it shows |
|-------|---------------|
| **Location Alerts** | Notifications when a care worker is not where they should be — not near the client, no clock-in, left the area, late arrival, or no response to a check |
| **Unfilled Events** | Scheduled shifts for the current week that have no worker assigned |

### Compliance, Quality & Billing

These panels help you manage the billing pipeline:

| Panel | What it shows |
|-------|---------------|
| **Timesheet Discrepancies** | Timesheets where the actual hours don't match the scheduled hours — review and resolve before approval |
| **Submitted Timesheets** | Timesheets that care workers have submitted and are waiting for your review |

### Navigating from the Dashboard

The Coordinate area also provides quick access to related pages via the sidebar:

- **Timesheets** — view and manage all timesheets
- **Timecards** — management time cards for coordination billing
- **Locations** — view care worker locations on a map
- **Location Alerts** — detailed view of all location alerts

### Daily Workflow

A typical daily workflow on the Coordinate Dashboard:

1. Check **Location Alerts** for any workers who missed clock-in or are not near their client
2. Review **Unfilled Events** to find and assign workers to open shifts
3. Review **Submitted Timesheets** to approve completed shifts
4. Investigate any **Timesheet Discrepancies** before approving

> **Tip:** The Location Alerts panel updates in real time. Keep the Coordinate Dashboard open during shift start times to catch issues as they happen.
