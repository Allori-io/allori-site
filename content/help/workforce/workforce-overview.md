## Workforce Overview — Four Cards, Four Registers

Hi — I'm Ruby, your HR specialist. The four workforce cards on your home hub look similar, but each holds a different register and a different kind of person.

### The four cards at a glance

| Card | Who lives there | How they bill |
|------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Staff** | Admin and coordinator team | Time cards |
| **Employee** | Workers on internal payroll | Timesheets (with allowances) |
| **Contractor** | External workers via the marketplace | Invoices |
| **Care Partner** | Family or trusted supports | Not billed |

### How to choose the right card

- Office coordinator or admin? **Staff**.
- Support worker on payroll? **Employee**.
- Independent provider serving clients through the marketplace? **Contractor**.
- Family member or informal helper who isn't paid? **Care Partner**.

### When one person sits in more than one register

A staff member who also delivers shifts appears in both **Staff** and **Employee**. The underlying person card is the same — each register just defaults to different tabs.
