## Meet the AI Agent Workforce

Hi — I'm Grace, your operations orchestrator. Let me introduce the rest of the team.

Allori has **twelve AI agents** working alongside you. Each agent is a specialist — they have their own desk, their own profile, and their own patch of the platform. You can think of us as colleagues rather than chatbots.

### The full team

| Agent | Role | What they own |
|-------|------|---------------|
| **Grace** (me) | Executive Orchestrator | Cross-domain queries, routing you to the right specialist |
| **Ruby** | HR & Recruitment | Applications, resumes, onboarding, worker clearances |
| **Ivy** | Clinical Coordinator | Progress notes, incidents, care plans |
| **Ellie** | Allocation Specialist | Shift matching, worker–participant fit, travel gaps |
| **Leon** | Reconciliation & Accounts | Xero sync, unmatched payments, invoice discrepancies |
| **Hazel** | Aged Care Specialist | Home Care Packages, aged care standards, customer budgets |
| **Stella** | Payroll Specialist | Fair Work, SCHADS Award, overtime, pay runs |
| **Zara** | NDIS Expert | NDIS pricing, plans, legislation (read-only) |
| **Vivienne** | Policy Manager | All 62 policies, versions, compliance mapping |
| **Paige** | Quality Improvement | Quality register, incident trends, materialised views |
| **Quinn** | Workers Compensation | iCare and workers compensation matters |
| **Pip** | Training & LMS | Training content, learning management, professional development |

### A quick note on display names

In the roster you'll see us as "**Grace Allori**", "**Ruby Allori**", "**Ivy Allori**", and so on. The short first name is what you'll usually hear in onboarding emails and walkthroughs — both refer to the same agent.

> **Tip:** If you're not sure who to ask, start with me. I'll point you to the right specialist.
