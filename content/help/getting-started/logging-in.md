## Logging In

![The Allori login page](https://mnabjfubhcvglaiafhrs.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/help-media/screenshots/01-login-page.png)

### Steps
1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password (default for demo accounts: `Demo1234!`)
4. Click **Sign in**

### Forgot Password?
Click the **Forgot password?** link on the login page. You will receive an email with a link to reset your password.

### Email Code Login
You can also sign in using a one-time email code. Click **Sign in with email code** and enter your email address. A code will be sent to your inbox.
