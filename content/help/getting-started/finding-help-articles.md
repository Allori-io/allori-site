## Finding Help Articles

Hi — I'm Grace, your operations orchestrator. Help is always two clicks away. Let me show you the quickest ways in.

### Two ways to reach help

| Entry point | Where it lives | When to use it |
|-------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Home hub search bar** | Top of your home page | You have a specific question in mind |
| **Help button** | Header or side navigation on every page | You want to browse the full help centre |

### Using the home hub search

1. Go to your **home hub** (click the Allori logo or the home icon).
2. Find the **search bar** at the top of the page.
3. Type a question or keyword — for example, "how do I add a client" or "timesheets".
4. Press **Enter** to see matching articles.

The home hub search covers every article we've written, plus FAQs and the staff and client registers. It's the single fastest way in.

### Using the Help button

The **Help** button is on the side navigation (or in the header on some screens). Clicking it takes you straight to the **Help Centre** at `/help`. From there you can browse by category, search again, or scan the popular articles.

### The help centre URL

If you ever want to bookmark it, the help centre lives at `/help`. Each article also has its own URL — for example, this article is at `/help/getting-started/finding-help-articles` — so you can share a link with a teammate.

> **Tip:** If you're reading an error message or working through a tricky page, look for the small **?** icon nearby first — that's contextual help, and it usually opens the most relevant article for where you are. See the "Using Contextual Help" article for details.
