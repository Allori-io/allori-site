## Navigating the Platform

![The Help Centre showing all categories](/help-media/02-help-centre.png)

### Main Navigation
The left sidebar contains all the main sections of the platform, organised by domain:

- **Coordinate** — Customers, employees, staff, suppliers, and their domains
- **Business Development** — Registrations, leads, prospects, and referral sources
- **Clinical** — Assessments, care plans, progress notes, and care diaries
- **Quality** — Feedback, incidents, risks, improvements, and audits
- **Accounts** — Receivables, payables, payments, and accounting
- **Human Resources** — Applications, applicants, recruitment, and interviews
- **Conversations** — Messaging threads across all domains
- **Agents** — AI agent workforce and tasks

### Finding People
The person register contains everyone in the system. Because it is very large, you cannot browse it as a list. Instead:

1. Press **Ctrl+K** (or click the search icon in the sidebar)
2. Type the person's name
3. Click the result to go to their person card

From their person card, you can click any role in the right sidebar to navigate to that domain (e.g., click "Customer" to go to their customer profile).

### Right Sidebar
Every person's profile shows a right sidebar with:
- Their photo
- Name and personal details
- All roles they hold in the system (clickable to navigate to that domain)
- Team members and emergency contacts (on recipient profiles)

### Help
Click the **?** icon on any page to access contextual help for that specific page.
