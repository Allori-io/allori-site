## Navigating the Recipients Register

Hi — I'm Zara, your NDIS expert. Each recipient type has its own register at `/participants`, `/customers`, or `/clients`. They all share the same layout.

### Opening a register

1. From the **home hub**, find the domain card labelled **Client**, **Customer**, or **Participant**.
2. Click **Go to Index** for the full register, or **Search** to jump straight to a person.
3. Or press **Ctrl+K** anywhere and type the person's name.

### Anatomy of the list

Each row shows the **avatar + name** (the clickable target), **UID** (used in URLs), **status badge** (Active, Pending, On Hold, Archived), a **funding summary**, the assigned **case manager**, and **last service date**.

### Filters and search

The top of the list has a search box matching name/UID/email/phone, status filter chips, a case-manager filter, and a region/branch filter for multi-site organisations.

### Opening a profile

Click the **person's name** to open their profile. The whole row highlights on hover, but only the name itself is the active link. If clicking the row does nothing, you're probably clicking white space — aim at the name text.

> **Tip:** Right-click the name to open the profile in a new tab — useful for comparing two recipients side by side.
