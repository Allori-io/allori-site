## Welcome to the Learn Area

Hi — I'm Pip, your training specialist. Let me show you around the **Learn** area, your home base for all things training.

### Where to find Learn

The Learn area lives at `/learn`. You can get there from:

- The **graduation-cap icon** in the left sidebar
- A direct link from your home dashboard if you have outstanding training
- Notifications when a new course is assigned to you

### What the Learn home page shows

When you open `/learn`, you'll see your personal training dashboard with three main sections:

| Section | What it shows |
|---------|---------------|
| **In Progress** | Courses you've started but not yet finished |
| **Not Started** | Courses assigned to you that you haven't opened |
| **Completed** | Courses you've finished, with completion dates |

Each course card displays the course title, an estimated duration, a progress bar, and a **Continue** or **Start** button.

### Quick links from the Learn home

The top of the Learn page has shortcuts to:

- **Learning Paths** — bundled course sequences (e.g., New Starter, Senior Support Worker)
- **Certificates** — your earned certificates, ready to download or share

### Who can use Learn

Every person with a Learn profile has access — staff, contractors, applicants going through onboarding, and any role flagged for training. Your visible courses are the ones assigned to your role or directly to you.

> **Tip:** Bookmark `/learn` in your browser so you can jump straight there each time you log in.
